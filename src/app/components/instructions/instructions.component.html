Instructions

The Lifestyle Incentive Program is Central 1's way of supporting individual efforts to maintain better health and fitness and recognizing our contributions to our workplace and communities. The program, available to all permanent full-time and part-time C1 employees, rewards employees for fitness, personal development, volunteerism, attendance, environmental responsibility and blood donations.

All reward points accumulate between January 1 and December 31 of each year.  Points are earned by participating in the Eligible Activities of your choice (see Eligible Activities tab) and recording those points over the course of the year.  You earn $80 for EACH point level achieved. The point levels are 100, 300, 500, 750 and 1,000.

MAXIMUM PAYOUT per employee per year is $400 (a total of 1,000 points).


Submission Instructions

Complete the form as the year progresses but make sure to have it all completed by December 31st at noon. All fields are required except "Notes".

Fields required to complete you record card for processing:

  • Employee Name
  • Department
  • Employee Number
  • Date of activity (Date)
  • Type of activity (Activity Code)
  • Points for activity (Points Earned)
  • The accumulating "TOTAL" points calculates automatically.

Activity Codes

  • A = Attendance
  • BD = Blood Donation
  • ER = Environmental Responsibility
  • F = Fitness
  • PD = Personal Development
  • V = Volunteer Activities
  • WL = Initial Weight Loss
  • WL6 = Weight Loss Maintained for 6 Months
  • WL12 = Weight Loss Maintained for 12 months
  • QS1 = Quit Smoking for 1 Month
  • QS3 = Quit Smoking for 3 Months
  • QS6 = Quit Smoking for 6 Months
  • QS2 = Quit Smoking for 12 Months

Weight Loss and Quit Smoking Levels

Weight Loss

  • For EACH achievement of initial weight loss, 6 months, and 12 months maintenance, you earn 200 points.
  • NOTE: the minimum eligible loss is 10lbs.

Quit Smoking

  • For EACH achievement of continuous non smoking intervals of 1, 3, 5, and 12 months, you earn 200 points.
  • NOTE: you must record your last date smoked.
